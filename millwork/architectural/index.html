AWI Quality Grades

Built to the Standard You Specify

Architectural Woodwork Institute (AWI) Quality Standards Illustrated, 2nd Edition. We bid and build to whichever grade is on your spec.

Economy Grade

Economy

Functional, paint-grade work where appearance is secondary. Service areas, utility spaces, light commercial.

  • MDF and pine substrates
  • Solid joinery
  • Paint-grade finish
Custom Grade

Custom (Default)

Our standard grade. Most spec residential and commercial work. Tighter tolerances, hardwood and veneer, dovetail or doweled joinery.

  • Plywood & hardwood substrates
  • Edge-banded or solid wood
  • Custom finish to sample
  • 3/64" tolerances
Premium Grade

Premium

High-end residential, executive office, hospitality flagship work. Rift-cut white oak, dovetailed solid drawers, museum-grade finish.

  • Rift / quartersawn veneer
  • Solid wood drawer boxes, dovetailed
  • Conversion varnish or catalyzed lacquer
  • 1/32" tolerances
