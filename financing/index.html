Common Questions

Financing FAQs

What financing options do you offer?
Two main programs: (1) 0% APR for 12 months — full project amount financed, no interest if paid in full within 12 months. (2) Same-as-cash for 90 days — short-term zero-interest for smaller projects. Both run a soft credit pull (no impact on your score) and approve in minutes.
How much will my monthly payment be?
Depends on project size. A $5,000 countertop project on the 0% APR for 12 months plan = ~$417/month. A $25,000 kitchen remodel on a 60-month extended plan = ~$420/month at competitive rates. Use the calculator above for a quick estimate, or we'll show exact numbers during your free consultation.
Will applying hurt my credit score?
No. The pre-qualification uses a soft credit pull which does not affect your credit score. Only if you proceed with the formal financing application after signing a contract is there a hard pull, and only one — not multiple inquiries.
Do you offer any cash discount?
Yes — we offer a small cash discount on projects paid in full upfront (no financing). Talk to your estimator about which approach makes sense for your budget.
Can I combine financing with current promotions?
Yes. Financing stacks with our Free Dinners promo and most other current offers. The only restriction is you can't combine financing with the cash discount above.
