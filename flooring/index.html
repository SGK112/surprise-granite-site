FAQ

Phoenix metro flooring, answered straight.

How much does flooring installation cost in the Phoenix metro?
Installed pricing in 2026: SPC and luxury vinyl plank (LVP) typically runs $5–$9 per sq ft installed. Engineered hardwood is $9–$16 per sq ft. Porcelain or ceramic tile is $9–$14 per sq ft. Natural stone (travertine, slate, marble) is $14–$24 per sq ft. We give a firm written quote after a free in-home measurement.
Is Lions Floor a good brand for Arizona homes?
Yes. We're an authorized Lions Floor installer because their SPC and WPC lines have a commercial-grade wear layer that handles Arizona's UV exposure, dog claws, and pool-area traffic without warping. Their Bambino, Grande, District, and Lone Star Spirit collections are our most-installed lines for kitchens, basements, and whole-home re-floors.
Do you install flooring outside of Surprise?
Yes — we serve the entire Phoenix metro. Our shop is in Surprise, AZ and we install in Sun City, Sun City West, Peoria, Glendale, Goodyear, Avondale, Litchfield Park, Vistancia, Phoenix, Scottsdale, Mesa, Chandler, and surrounding West and East Valley communities. Each city has its own page with drive time and HOA notes.
Can I get free dinners with a flooring job?
Yes. The 3 Free Dinners promo applies to any new flooring project, new countertops, or a full kitchen/bath remodel. We send three a partner local restaurant gift cards the day your install starts. Phoenix metro, 2026 promotion.
How long does flooring installation take?
Most single-room SPC or LVP installs take 1–2 days. Whole-home LVP runs 3–5 days depending on square footage. Tile takes longer because of mortar cure time — figure 3–7 days for a kitchen or large bath. Hardwood install + finish runs 4–6 days. We schedule install crews back-to-back so we don't stretch your project.
