About Cyrus Kardigan

Cyrus Kardigan Luxury Vinyl Planks features light gray tones, with soft cream and blue-ish hues for an on-trend reclaimed look. This waterproof vinyl plank flooring features a pre-attached 1MM pad for comfort and quiet underfoot. It is 100% waterproof and contains CrystaLux™, an industry-leading wear layer that guards against everyday scratches and depreciation. It's available in both 7\" x48\" and 9\" x60\" in sizes for design flexibility. A patented locking system allows for easy installation and is ideal for hassle-free installation over existing flooring.

This premium flooring is available at Surprise Granite in Arizona. Visit our showroom or request a free estimate to see this beautiful flooring in person.

Care & Maintenance

Proper care will keep your Cyrus Kardigan flooring looking beautiful for years:

  • Sweep or vacuum regularly to remove dirt and debris
  • Mop with a damp mop and mild cleaner
  • Wipe up spills promptly
  • Use furniture pads to prevent scratches
  • Avoid harsh chemicals and abrasive cleaners
