About Trecento Windsor Crest

Bold black and white floral patterns in various shapes and sizes highlight the unique beauty of Trecento Windsor Crest Luxury Vinyl Tile. The 12 \"x24\" waterproof vinyl flooring is 100% waterproof, scratch, stain, and dent resistant.

Care & Maintenance

Proper care will keep your Trecento Windsor Crest flooring looking beautiful for years:

  • Sweep or vacuum regularly to remove dirt and debris
  • Mop with a damp mop and mild cleaner
  • Wipe up spills promptly
  • Use furniture pads to prevent scratches
  • Avoid harsh chemicals and abrasive cleaners
