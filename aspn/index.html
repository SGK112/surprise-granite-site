About

Founded by Surprise Granite

Surprise Granite (AZ ROC #341113) is the founding operator. After years of fabricating countertops for homes across the Phoenix metro, we kept hearing the same thing — homeowners couldn't find good stone-trade pros, and good stone-trade pros couldn't find each other. ASPN is the network that fixes both.

ASPN is intentionally not a marketing program for Surprise Granite. Members are not required to use SG for fabrication or anything else. We're the operators because somebody had to build it; we're not the only fabricator at the table.

Founding seats are free and limited.
