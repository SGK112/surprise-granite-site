Print Specs

  • Business cards: Square version at 0.6"–0.75" wide. Place on the back, bottom corner.
  • Truck wraps & yard signs: SVGs scale to any size — 12"+ for vehicles, 24"+ for signs.
  • Email signature: Wide version, max-width 240px. The PNG is best for email clients.
  • Social profile: Square version as a profile sticker overlay or pinned post.

Usage Rules

  • Only approved ASPN members may display the badge. If your application was rejected or your membership lapsed, please remove it from your materials.
  • Do not modify the badge artwork (no recoloring, cropping, or adding your own logo on top).
  • The web badge must link to your verified profile or to https://www.surprisegranite.com/aspn/.
  • Print uses fine — no link required for cards, trucks, signs, etc.
  • Misuse may result in revoked membership and a takedown request.
