Common Questions

Tile Shower & Bath FAQs

How much does a custom tile shower cost in Phoenix?
Custom tile shower installations in Phoenix metro typically run $5,500–$15,000 for the shower alone. Full bathroom remodels including the shower, vanity, tile floor, fixtures, and lighting run $15,000–$45,000. Frameless glass adds $1,200–$3,500. Walk-in tub conversions $7,500–$14,000. Fixed quote in writing after our in-home measure.
Curbless vs traditional tile shower?
Curbless (zero-entry) showers eliminate the step-over threshold, look modern, and age in place. They require sloped subfloor work and a linear drain — adds about $1,500–$3,000 vs a standard curb. Standard curb showers are still our most-installed style in Sun City and Sun City Grand because they're simpler and faster.
How long does a bathroom remodel take?
Shower-only replacement: 7-10 working days. Full bathroom remodel: 2-4 weeks depending on layout changes. We schedule plumbing and tile work back-to-back so the bathroom isn't out of commission longer than necessary. We often arrange a portable shower if you have only one bathroom.
Do you handle walk-in tub conversions?
Yes — common request in Sun City, Sun City West, and Sun City Grand. We replace traditional tubs with walk-in tubs, walk-in showers, or curbless walk-in showers. Plumbing relocation, tile, fixtures, lighting included. Most conversions complete in 5-7 days on-site.
Can you install niches, benches, and seats?
Yes. Recessed niches, built-in benches, foldable shower seats, accent tile bands, dual shower heads, body sprays, rain heads, hand showers — all standard inclusions on custom shower builds. We design these in during the consultation.
