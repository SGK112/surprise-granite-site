Common Questions

Remodeling FAQs

What does a full kitchen and bath remodel cost in Phoenix?
Full kitchen remodels in Phoenix metro typically run $35,000 to $90,000 depending on cabinet tier, countertop material, and scope of layout changes. Full bathroom remodels run $15,000 to $45,000. A combined kitchen + bath project usually lands in the $50,000–$120,000 range with our Full Course Remodel pricing — one contract, one crew, one schedule.
How long does a full remodel take?
A standard kitchen remodel runs 4–7 weeks from demo to final walkthrough. A bathroom remodel runs 2–4 weeks. Doing both simultaneously usually shaves 2–3 weeks off the combined timeline because we sequence trades efficiently and mobilize once.
Do you handle the whole project or just countertops/cabinets?
Whole project. Demo, framing changes, electrical, plumbing trim, drywall, paint, cabinets, countertops, tile, flooring, lighting, hardware. We sub the trades we don't keep in-house but you sign one contract with us, get one schedule, and have one point of accountability.
Are you licensed and insured?
Yes. AZ ROC #341113. Fully insured with general liability. Certificate of insurance available on request — most HOAs and commercial clients require it before work starts and we deliver within 24 hours.
What areas do you serve?
Entire Phoenix metro: Surprise (HQ), Peoria, Sun City, Sun City West, Glendale, Phoenix, Scottsdale, Paradise Valley, Mesa, Chandler, Gilbert, Goodyear, Avondale, Buckeye, Litchfield Park.
Can I do this in phases?
Yes. Some clients do kitchen first, then bath six months later, then flooring after that. We can sequence work across multiple project phases on a single master plan so each phase fits the previous one without rework.
