## What Is Dekton?

Dekton is an ultra-compact surface manufactured by Cosentino, the same company behind Silestone quartz. What sets Dekton apart is its unique manufacturing process called TSP (Technology of Sintered Particles). This process subjects raw materials to extreme heat (over 1,200Â°C) and pressure, simulating in hours what nature takes thousands of years to create.

The result is an incredibly dense, durable surface with properties that exceed natural stone, quartz, and even porcelain.

## Why Dekton Is Perfect for Arizona

### Extreme Heat Resistance

This is where Dekton truly shines. While quartz countertops can be damaged by hot pots and pans, Dekton laughs at heat. You can place a scorching pan directly from the stove onto your Dekton countertop without worrying about thermal shock, burns, or discoloration.

For Arizona's outdoor kitchens where grill temperatures soar, Dekton is the ultimate solution.

### UV Stability

Arizona averages over 300 sunny days per year. That intense desert sun can fade and discolor many countertop materials over time. Dekton is completely UV stable - it won't yellow, fade, or change color even with years of direct sun exposure.

This makes Dekton ideal for:
- Outdoor kitchen countertops
- Pool area bars and prep stations
- Indoor countertops near large windows
- Covered patio surfaces

### Incredible Scratch Resistance

Dekton is one of the hardest countertop materials available. It resists scratches from knives, abrasive cleaners, and daily wear better than granite, quartz, or marble. You can actually cut directly on a Dekton surface without damage (though we still recommend a cutting board to protect your knives).

### Zero Porosity

Like quartz, Dekton is non-porous and doesn't require sealing. Liquids, oils, and stains can't penetrate the surface. Even harsh chemicals like bleach won't damage Dekton.

### Freeze-Thaw Resistant

While Arizona rarely freezes, those occasional cold winter nights won't affect your outdoor Dekton countertops. The material handles temperature extremes in both directions.

## Installation Considerations

### Thickness Options

Dekton comes in multiple thicknesses:
- **8mm (0.3"):** Ideal for wall cladding and backsplashes
- **12mm (0.5"):** Great for countertops and tables
- **20mm (0.8"):** Standard countertop thickness
- **30mm (1.2"):** Premium thickness for dramatic edges

### Large Format Slabs

Dekton slabs can be manufactured in very large sizes, allowing for seamless installations with minimal joints. This is especially attractive for waterfall islands and long stretches of countertop.

### Professional Installation Required

Due to its unique properties, Dekton requires experienced fabricators. Our certified installation team at Surprise Granite has the training and equipment to ensure your Dekton countertops are installed perfectly.
