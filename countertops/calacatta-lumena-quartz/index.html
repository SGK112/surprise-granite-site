About Calacatta Lumena

Calacatta Lumena displays a cool, serene whiteness, flowing with understated grey veining that offers the look of a gentle watercolor brush stroke. This quartz embodies a tranquil yet moving aesthetic, with each vein creating a soft gradient that fades into the background, perfect for creating a soothing and luxurious atmosphere in any space.

Care & Maintenance

Proper care will keep your Calacatta Lumena countertop looking beautiful for decades. Follow these guidelines:

💧

Daily Cleaning

Use a soft cloth with warm water and mild dish soap. Avoid abrasive cleaners.

🛡

Sealing

Natural stone should be sealed periodically for optimal protection.

⚠

Avoid Harsh Chemicals

Acidic cleaners can damage the surface. Use pH-neutral products.

🍴

Use Protection

Always use cutting boards and trivets to protect the surface.
