Countertop Installation FAQ

How long does countertop installation take? +
Most countertop installations are completed in one day. The full process from template to installation typically takes 2-3 weeks, including material ordering and fabrication.
Do I need to be home during countertop installation? +
Yes, someone 18 or older should be present during installation. The process usually takes 2-4 hours depending on kitchen size and complexity.
Will my countertops have seams? +
Most installations require seams due to slab size limitations and transport. Our fabricators place seams strategically to minimize visibility, often near sinks or in low-traffic areas.
How do I prepare for countertop installation? +
Clear countertops completely, disconnect plumbing if DIY, ensure clear access to work areas, and remove fragile items from nearby cabinets. We'll provide a detailed prep checklist.
