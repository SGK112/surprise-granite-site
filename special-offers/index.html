Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our deals and specials.

What are remnant countertops? +

Remnants are leftover pieces from larger slab cuts. They are the same premium-quality granite, quartz, or marble, just in smaller sizes. They are perfect for bathroom vanities, laundry rooms, wet bars, fireplace surrounds, and small kitchen islands. Because they would otherwise go unused, we pass the savings on to you — starting at just $25 per square foot installed.

How do I know if a remnant will fit my project? +

Call us at (602) 833-3189 with your measurements and we will check our current inventory for pieces that fit. Remnants typically work best for projects under 25 square feet. Our team can also visit your home for a free measurement.

Can I combine multiple offers? +

Some promotions can be combined. For example, you can use our financing option with any current promotion. Bundle deals already include built-in savings and cannot be combined with other percentage-off offers. Call us to discuss the best combination for your project.

How does the 0% APR financing work? +

Qualified customers can get 0% APR financing for 12 months on any remodeling project. The approval process takes just minutes. There are no hidden fees and no prepayment penalties. Monthly payments start as low as $50 depending on your project total.

How long are these special offers available? +

Most promotions run through the end of the current month, but availability depends on remaining inventory and booking capacity. The $500 Off Kitchen Remodel is limited to the first 5 customers each month. We recommend calling early to lock in your deal.
