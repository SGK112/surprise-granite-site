Tile Shower Remodel Questions

Common questions about our tile shower remodeling services.

Tile shower remodel costs in Phoenix range from $4,000-$15,000+ depending on shower size, tile selection, and features. Basic shower tile starts around $4,000. Custom designs with niches, benches, and premium tile run $8,000-$12,000. Luxury builds with large-format porcelain, linear drains, and frameless glass start at $12,000+.
A typical tile shower remodel takes 5-10 days from demolition to completion. This includes demo (1 day), waterproofing (1-2 days), tile installation (2-4 days), and grouting/sealing (1 day). Frameless glass enclosures are measured after tile and installed within 1-2 weeks.
We use Schluter-KERDI membrane and RedGard liquid waterproofing systems. Proper waterproofing is the most critical step in any shower tile project - it prevents moisture from reaching your home's framing and subfloor. Every shower we build includes full waterproofing behind all tiled surfaces.
Yes, tub-to-shower conversions are one of our most popular services. We remove the tub, reconfigure plumbing, install waterproofing and a new shower pan, then build your custom tile shower. Most tub-to-shower conversions take 5-7 days and start at $6,000.
Porcelain tile is the most popular choice for showers due to its low water absorption rate, durability, and wide range of styles. Large-format porcelain (12x24 or larger) creates a modern look with fewer grout lines. Ceramic tile is a budget-friendly option. Natural stone like marble or travertine adds luxury but requires regular sealing.
Yes. We coordinate frameless, semi-frameless, and framed glass enclosures with our shower tile installations. Glass is measured after tile work is complete for a perfect fit. Frameless glass provides a sleek, modern look and is easier to keep clean than framed options.
