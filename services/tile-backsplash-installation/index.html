FAQ

Tile Installation Questions

Common questions about our tile and backsplash installation services.

Tile backsplash installation in Phoenix typically costs $8-$25 per square foot installed, depending on tile type and pattern complexity. A standard kitchen backsplash (30-40 sq ft) runs $300-$1,000 for installation plus tile cost. Subway tile patterns are most affordable; intricate mosaics cost more.
Most kitchen backsplash installations are completed in 1-2 days. Day one involves tile setting, and day two is for grouting and sealing. Larger projects or complex patterns may take 2-3 days. The tile needs 24 hours to cure before grouting.
Yes, we recommend removing old backsplash for the best results. Our installation service includes removal and disposal of existing backsplash, wall preparation, and new tile installation. Installing over old tile can create thickness issues with outlets and cabinets.
Ceramic and porcelain tiles are most popular for kitchen backsplashes due to durability and easy cleaning. Glass tile adds visual depth. Natural stone like marble or travertine offers luxury appeal but requires sealing. We help you choose based on your style, budget, and maintenance preferences.
Yes, we install tile in showers, bathroom floors, tub surrounds, and accent walls. Shower tile requires proper waterproofing and slope for drainage. We use Schluter or RedGard waterproofing systems to protect against moisture damage and ensure long-lasting installations.
Yes, we install floor tile throughout homes including kitchens, bathrooms, entryways, laundry rooms, and living areas. Floor tile installation costs $8-$15 per square foot for labor, plus tile cost. We handle subfloor preparation, leveling, and transitions to other flooring types.
