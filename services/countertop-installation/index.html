FAQ

Countertop Installation Questions

Everything you need to know about our countertop fabrication and installation services.

Most countertop installations are completed in one day. The full process from estimate to installation typically takes 7-10 business days, including templating and fabrication. Complex projects with multiple seams or special features may take slightly longer.
Our all-inclusive pricing covers: digital laser templating, custom fabrication, sink cutouts, standard edge profile, installation, sealing (for natural stone), removal and disposal of old countertops, and a lifetime warranty on workmanship. Plumbing disconnect/reconnect is also included.
Yes, removal and disposal of your existing countertops is included in our standard installation service at no extra charge. Our team will carefully remove old countertops, disconnect plumbing fixtures, and haul away all debris.
We offer a variety of edge profiles including: Eased (straight edge with rounded corners), Beveled, Bullnose (full round), Half Bullnose, Ogee, Waterfall, and Mitered edges. Standard eased edge is included in the price; premium edges are available for an additional charge.
Clear all items from your existing countertops and the area below the sink. Ensure a clear path from the entry to your kitchen. If you have pets, please secure them in another room. We'll handle disconnecting plumbing and appliances. Someone 18+ should be present during installation.
Seams may be necessary depending on your countertop layout and slab size. We strategically place seams in the least visible locations and use color-matched epoxy to make them as inconspicuous as possible. L-shaped and U-shaped kitchens typically require seams at corners.
Surprise Granite provides a lifetime warranty on our workmanship and installation. Material warranties vary by manufacturer: most quartz brands offer 10-15 year warranties, while natural stone warranties vary. We also offer optional StoneGuard Pro protection plans.
Countertop prices in Phoenix range from $40-$150 per square foot installed, depending on material. Granite: $45-$80/sq ft, Quartz: $55-$120/sq ft, Marble: $60-$150/sq ft, Porcelain: $50-$90/sq ft. Average kitchen (30 sq ft) costs $1,500-$4,500 installed.
