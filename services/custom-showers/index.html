Custom Shower Questions

Common questions about our custom shower installation services.

Custom shower costs in Phoenix range from $4,000-$15,000+ depending on size, tile selection, and features. A basic tiled shower starts around $4,000-$6,000. Mid-range showers with niches and benches run $6,000-$10,000. High-end showers with premium tile, multiple niches, linear drains, and frameless glass run $10,000-$15,000+.
A complete custom shower build typically takes 5-10 days depending on complexity. This includes demolition (1 day), waterproofing and pan installation (1-2 days), tile installation (2-4 days), grouting and sealing (1 day), and glass installation (scheduled separately, usually within a week).
We use industry-leading waterproofing systems including Schluter-KERDI and RedGard. Schluter systems provide a bonded waterproof membrane under the tile, while RedGard is a liquid-applied membrane. Both prevent water damage to your home's structure. Proper waterproofing is the most critical part of any shower build.
Yes, tub-to-shower conversions are one of our most popular services. We remove the existing tub, reroute plumbing as needed, install proper waterproofing and drainage, and build your new custom shower. Most conversions take 5-7 days to complete.
Yes, we install frameless, semi-frameless, and framed glass shower enclosures. Frameless glass provides a modern, open look and is easier to clean. We work with local glass companies to measure and install after tile work is complete. Glass is typically installed within a week of tile completion.
Absolutely. Built-in shower benches and recessed niches are popular features we include in custom showers. Benches provide seating and can be corner or floating style. Niches offer storage for shampoo and soap. We can also add multiple niches at different heights for convenience.
