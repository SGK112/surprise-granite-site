FAQ

Refacing questions, answered.

How much does cabinet refacing cost in Phoenix?
Refacing an average Phoenix kitchen runs $5,000–$12,000 installed, about 40–60% less than new. Price depends on door style, material (thermofoil, veneer, solid wood), and cabinet count. Free written quote at your in-home measure.
Can I reface laminate or thermofoil cabinets?
Yes, if the boxes are sound and the faces aren't delaminated beyond repair. We inspect during the free measure and tell you straight whether refacing is the right move or if new boxes make more sense.
Will my kitchen be unusable?
No — that's the big win over new cabinets. You keep using the space through most of the project. Doors go off, new ones go on. Only the sink area has a few hours of downtime during the countertop swap if you're doing both.
Do you also replace the countertops?
Most of our refacing customers do. We template and install countertops at the same time so the kitchen comes back in one go. See countertop options.
