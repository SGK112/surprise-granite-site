The Full Process

6 Phases, 5–10 Weeks Total

Most kitchen remodels run 5–7 weeks. Bath-only is shorter (2–4 weeks). Custom cabinets push longer (up to 10 weeks). Below is the standard path.

1
Day 1 · Free In-Home Estimate

We Come to You

60–90 minute visit. A designer arrives with cabinet door samples, stone chips, tile samples, and a laser measure. We measure your space, talk through what you want, and give a verbal pricing range on the spot.

  • Door samples in 4–6 styles you preferred
  • Stone chips for your color preferences
  • Tile and flooring options coordinated to your stone
  • Layout walk-through, work-zone analysis
  • Verbal pricing range — not "starting at"
What this isn't: A high-pressure sales pitch. We quote, leave, let you decide.
2
Week 1 · Renderings & Written Quote

You See It Before You Sign

Within 5–7 business days we deliver a written line-item quote AND 3D renderings of your finished space. Kitchen elevations, perspective views, finish boards. You see the project before committing.

  • Fixed line-item quote (not "estimate")
  • 3D renderings: elevations + perspective
  • Finish board with your samples
  • Project timeline draft
  • Financing pre-qualification offered (soft credit pull)
3
Week 2 · Sign & Schedule

One Signature Locks Everything

Sign the contract, deposit, materials ordered. Project schedule locks in. From here, we own the timeline.

  • Signed contract (matches the quote — no surprise add-ons)
  • Deposit cleared
  • Materials ordered same day
  • Templating & install dates calendared
  • HOA paperwork submitted (if needed)
4
Weeks 2–6 · Materials Lead Time

We Build, You Wait

Cabinet manufacturing and stone slab procurement happen in parallel. You don't lift a finger. We give weekly status updates by text or email — your preference.

  • Stock cabinets: 2–3 week lead time
  • Semi-custom cabinets: 4–6 weeks
  • Fully custom cabinets: 6–10 weeks
  • Stone slab: hand-selected at the yard, delivered to our shop
  • Tile and flooring stocked or ordered to arrive same day as install
5
Final 1–3 Weeks · Demo & Build

The Loud Part

On-site work begins. Floors and surfaces protected. Demo, electrical, plumbing trim, drywall, cabinet install, countertop template + install, tile, flooring, hardware. We clean up every day before leaving.

  • Day 1–2: Demo + utility prep
  • Day 3–5: Cabinet install
  • Day 5–7: Countertop template (laser)
  • Day 7–10: Stone fabrication in our shop
  • Day 10–12: Countertop install (typically 2–3 hours)
  • Day 10–14: Tile, backsplash, flooring
  • Day 14–18: Final fixtures, hardware, paint touch-up
If your kitchen is your only kitchen: we set up a temp coffee/microwave station. Your Free Dinners kick in here.
6
Final Day · Punch & Walkthrough

Sign-Off & Keys Back

Final walk-through together. We document any punch items and resolve them within 48 hours. Final cleaning. Warranty packet, care instructions, attic stock delivered.

  • Walkthrough with project lead
  • Punch list: anything < perfect documented
  • 48-hour punch resolution guarantee
  • Warranty packet (workmanship + manufacturer)
  • Care & maintenance instructions
  • Attic stock (extra cabinet door, hardware, touch-up paint)
  • Photos for your records
