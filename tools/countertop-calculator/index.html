Measuring Tips

1

Measure in inches - Measure the length along the wall and the depth from wall to front edge.

2

Add sections for L or U shapes - Break complex layouts into rectangular sections for accurate calculation.

3

Standard depth is 25" - Most kitchen countertops are 25 inches deep, including the overhang.

4

Include islands separately - Add a new section for kitchen islands with their own measurements.
