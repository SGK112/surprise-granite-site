Why Glendale Homeowners Hire Us for Flooring

Glendale flooring conversation maps to housing era. Arrowhead Ranch and Hillcrest Ranch (1990s/2000s) typically have tile floors and carpeted bedrooms — homeowners want to swap to hardwood-look LVP or SPC for cleaning ease and visual continuity. We install all of it — SPC, LVP, WPC, hardwood, tile, natural stone — from our Surprise shop, 12–18 minutes away.

Arrowhead Ranch carpet-to-LVP swap

The most-quoted Glendale flooring job. Take out 1990s/2000s carpet in living rooms, hallways, bedrooms; install Lions Floor SPC or LVP throughout. Visual continuity from kitchen tile through living areas. Average Glendale home (~1,500 sq ft of new flooring): $9,000–$14,000 installed.

Tile-to-LVP transitions

Many Glendale homes have 12-inch or 16-inch tile in the wet areas but carpet everywhere else. We can either keep the tile and transition to LVP or replace everything with one continuous SPC product line for monolithic look. Both work.

Catlin Court historic preservation

Pre-1960 Catlin Court wood-floor refinishing. Sand to bare wood, restain, three coats poly. Saves the original character; LVP-over-historic-wood is rarely the right call here.

Glendale Flooring FAQs

How much does flooring cost in Glendale per square foot?
Glendale 2026 installed pricing: Lions Floor SPC/LVP $5–9/sq ft, hardwood $9–16/sq ft, engineered hardwood $9–14/sq ft, tile (porcelain) $7–12/sq ft, natural stone $14–28/sq ft, carpet $4–7/sq ft. Includes subfloor prep, vapor barrier where required, transitions, and trim.
Do you install Lions Floor in Glendale?
Yes — we're an authorized Lions Floor installer for Phoenix metro. Bambino, BevelMark, Grande, District, Lone Star Spirit, Canyon Coast, and the rest of the Lions Floor product line. Quote during in-home estimate.
Can you install over my existing Glendale tile?
Often, yes. SPC, LVP, and WPC float over level tile, hardwood, vinyl, or concrete. We assess flatness during the in-home estimate. Out-of-level tile may need self-leveling pour first; we'll flag and quote during the visit.
How long is install for a Glendale home?
1,000 sq ft completes in 2–3 days for SPC/LVP. Larger homes 4–5 days. Hardwood adds 1 day for staining/finish drying. Tile is slower — 5–7 days for typical kitchen + bath floor area. We can sequence rooms so you don't move out.
HOA approval for Glendale flooring changes?
Most interior flooring work doesn't require HOA approval. Arrowhead, Stetson Valley, Marbella Vineyards HOAs may require approval for floor changes that affect sound transmission to neighbors (condos, attached units). We provide documentation if asked.
Best flooring for Glendale dogs and pool wear?
Lions Floor SPC commercial-grade wear layer. We've installed in Glendale homes with multiple dogs and pool walk-in patterns — survives both. Avoid wood (claw scratches) or laminate (water damage from wet feet) in pool-adjacent rooms.
