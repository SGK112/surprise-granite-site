Common Questions

Mesa Cabinet FAQs

How much do custom cabinets cost in Mesa?
Mesa 2026 cabinet pricing: stock $4,000–$8,000, semi-custom $10,000–$20,000, fully custom $20,000–$45,000 for an average 25-linear-foot kitchen. Refacing $4,500–$9,000. Our West Valley shop is 50 minutes via loop 202 from Mesa; no Phoenix-overhead markup on your quote.
Do you serve all of Mesa?
Yes — Las Sendas, Eastmark, Red Mountain Ranch, Augusta Ranch, Mesa Grande, and the rest of Mesa. 50 minutes via Loop 202 from our Surprise shop via Loop 202 / Loop 303.
Can you handle Mesa HOA paperwork?
Yes. We provide AZ ROC #341113, certificate of insurance, scope of work, and start/finish dates. We've worked through most Mesa HOAs.
What's popular in Mesa kitchens right now?
Painted shaker in white and greige dominates. Soft-close everywhere. Quartz waterfall islands common in newer Eastmark builds; traditional raised panel still strong in older East Mesa homes.
Why hire a Surprise contractor for a Mesa kitchen?
Honest answer: most Mesa homeowners shouldn't, for small jobs. For full-kitchen or multi-trade scopes, we're often more competitive on price than Mesa-overhead contractors and we self-perform 3–4 trades the local guy subs out. That cost difference usually exceeds our travel.
Do you offer reduced rates given the drive?
Our rates are the same Phoenix-metro-wide. We don't add a Mesa surcharge, and we don't discount — both create accountability problems. The economics work because we self-perform multiple trades on each project.
Can you do remote consultations to skip an in-person estimate?
Yes for repeat work or simple scopes. We do video walk-throughs over FaceTime/Zoom for Mesa first contacts, with a follow-up in-person measure once you're committed.
