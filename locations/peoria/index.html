Common Questions

Peoria FAQs

Do you remodel in Vistancia and Trilogy at Vistancia?
Yes — both are core service areas. Vistancia and Trilogy at Vistancia are 8–10 minutes from our Surprise showroom. We've done full kitchens, master baths, custom millwork, and whole-home flooring throughout both communities. The Vistancia Community Association ARC packet is templated; we file it for you.
What's the most popular Peoria flooring upgrade?
24x48 wood-look porcelain tile through main living areas, with engineered hardwood or premium LVP in bedrooms. It mimics the look of wide-plank hardwood but holds up to AZ heat, dog claws, and the occasional pool-water track-in. We install around 12,000–18,000 sq ft of it per year in Peoria alone.
Do you build custom cabinets for Peoria homes?
Yes — full custom and semi-custom. We mill in our Surprise shop. Common Peoria builds: tall pantry walls for Vistancia floor plans, painted shaker islands, beverage center built-ins, garage cabinetry, and home-office wall units. Lead time is typically 4–8 weeks from design sign-off.
Can you do natural stone countertops in Peoria?
Yes. We carry granite, quartzite, marble, soapstone, and travertine slabs at our partner stone yards (Arizona Tile, MSI). Peoria customers walk a partner yard in person, mark the exact slab, and watch us template, fabricate, and install. Most full-kitchen counter jobs run $3,500–$8,500.
How long does a Peoria kitchen remodel take?
Typical timeline: 3–5 weeks from demo to final walkthrough. Refacing-only: 1 week. Counters-only: 2–3 weeks (template, fabricate, install). Full custom kitchen with cabinets, counters, tile, and electrical: 4–6 weeks. We give you a printed schedule on day one and stick to it.
How quickly can you start a project after I sign?
From signed contract: refacing 3 weeks, semi-custom cabinets 5–7 weeks, fully custom 6–10 weeks, countertop installation 2–3 weeks, tile shower 1–2 weeks. We don't double-book — your dates hold once we schedule.
Do you handle countertops, cabinets, AND tile, or just one?
All of it, plus flooring, millwork, and full kitchen and bath remodels. Self-performing multiple trades is the reason our pricing is competitive — you don't pay for trade coordination across separate contractors.
How does payment and financing work?
Standard schedule is 30% at contract, 40% at install start, 30% at completion. We accept cash, credit, check, and offer financing through our finance partners — most projects can be financed in 1–2 minutes during the in-home estimate.
Are you licensed and insured?
Yes — Arizona ROC #341113, fully bonded and insured. Our installation crews are background-checked. We provide license, insurance certificate, and scope of work in writing before any work starts.
