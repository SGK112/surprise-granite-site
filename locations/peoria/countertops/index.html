Why Peoria Homeowners Hire Us for Countertops

Common Questions

Peoria Countertop FAQs

How much do quartz countertops cost in Peoria?
Peoria 2026 quartz pricing: $3,500–$6,500 installed for a typical 40 sq ft kitchen. Includes slab, fabrication in our Surprise shop, edge profile, sink cutout, undermount install, tear-out and haul-away of existing counters.
Do you serve north Peoria — Vistancia, Trilogy, Westwing?
Yes. North Peoria 85383 is 18–25 minutes from our Surprise shop via Loop 303. We schedule in-home estimates Monday–Saturday and prioritize same-week measure visits.
Can I just replace my island counter and keep the rest?
Yes. Island-only or perimeter-only replacements are common, especially in Trilogy and Vistancia. We'll quote partial scope and we'll be honest if mixing old and new will look mismatched (rare with quartz, more common with natural stone).
What about a kitchen project while we're traveling?
Many Peoria homeowners — especially Trilogy snowbirds — schedule counter work during travel. We work with key access, daily photo updates, and a final walkthrough on your return. We carry coverage for unattended-home work.
Do you slab-match for veined quartzite or marble?
Yes. For book-matched or sequence-matched veined slabs (quartzite, marble, large-format porcelain), we visit the partner stone yard with you, mark your slabs, and lay them out in the shop before cutting. Large-format Neolith and Dekton sequencing requires the same approach.
How does Peoria's monsoon affect counter install?
We don't deliver or install during active monsoon storms — humidity and rain mess with epoxy seam-joining. We watch the radar and reschedule by 24 hours when needed; this is rare but always free of charge.
