Why Chandler Homeowners Hire Us for Countertops

Chandler is at the eastern edge of our practical service radius — 45–55 minutes from our Surprise shop. Chandler Countertop FAQs

Are you actually serving Chandler or just listing it?
We do serve Chandler — but honestly: small jobs probably want a closer Chandler contractor. For full kitchen counters or multi-trade scopes (counter + cabinet + tile), we're often more competitive on price than Chandler-overhead contractors, even with the travel.
Pricing for a Chandler kitchen?
Same as our wider service area: $3,500–$6,500 quartz, $3,000–$7,000 granite, $5,500–$11,000 quartzite. We don't add a Chandler surcharge.
Lead time?
Add 3–5 days vs our closer service area to allow for delivery scheduling. Typical: 3–4 weeks contract to finished counters.
Do you slab-select at Chandler-area yards?
Yes — Arizona Tile in Tempe, MSI Surfaces, and others are 15–20 minutes from Chandler. We meet you there for slab selection.
Permits?
Counter-only swaps usually don't need a Chandler permit. Anything affecting plumbing, electrical, gas, or walls does. We pull in our license name.
Can we do video-walkthrough first instead of in-home estimate?
Yes. We do video walkthroughs over FaceTime/Zoom for first contacts in Chandler, with a follow-up in-person template once you're committed. Saves us both an unnecessary trip if it's not a fit.
