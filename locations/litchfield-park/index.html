Common Questions

Litchfield Park FAQs

Do you remodel historic Wigwam-area homes?
Yes — historic and luxury homes around the Wigwam Resort are some of our favorite projects. Custom millwork, hardwood refinishing, marble baths, real natural stone, and one-off design work that respects the home's character. We've worked across Old Litchfield, Wigwam Creek, and the Russell Ranch / Dreaming Summit corridor.
What's a typical Litchfield Park kitchen budget?
Most Litchfield Park kitchens are higher-end: $35,000–$95,000. Custom cabinets + quartzite + premium hardware: $45,000–$80,000. Full luxury remodel with custom millwork, hardwood floors, designer lighting: $80,000+. We bring slab samples and full design renderings to the in-home consultation.
Can you do real hardwood flooring in Litchfield Park?
Yes — engineered hardwood is the AZ-stable choice for Litchfield Park's mix of historic and new builds. Wide-plank white oak, walnut, hickory in 5–9 inch planks. We also refinish original hardwood in older Litchfield Park homes when the floors are sound.
Do you handle the Litchfield Park HOA process?
Yes. Litchfield Park communities (especially Russell Ranch and Dreaming Summit) take their architectural review seriously. We provide AZ ROC #341113, certificate of insurance, project drawings, and the timeline. We've gone through it many times and keep the docs moving.
Do you work on Old Litchfield character homes?
Yes — we love the older Litchfield Park homes. They often have original details (built-ins, archways, plaster walls) worth preserving. We work with them, not against them, and our custom millwork shop can match historic moldings and panel profiles.
How quickly can you start a project after I sign?
From signed contract: refacing 3 weeks, semi-custom cabinets 5–7 weeks, fully custom 6–10 weeks, countertop installation 2–3 weeks, tile shower 1–2 weeks. We don't double-book — your dates hold once we schedule.
Do you handle countertops, cabinets, AND tile, or just one?
All of it, plus flooring, millwork, and full kitchen and bath remodels. Self-performing multiple trades is the reason our pricing is competitive.
How does payment and financing work?
Standard schedule is 30% at contract, 40% at install start, 30% at completion. We accept cash, credit, check, and offer financing through our finance partners.
Are you licensed and insured?
Yes — Arizona ROC #341113, fully bonded and insured. Crews are background-checked. We provide license, insurance certificate, and scope of work in writing.
