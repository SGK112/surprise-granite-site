Common Questions

Sun City FAQs

What's the closest contractor to Sun City for kitchen and bath remodels?
Surprise Granite, headquartered at 15464 W Aster Dr in Surprise — about 5 miles from central Sun City. We've remodeled hundreds of Sun City homes and know the floor plans inside out. Closer = faster response on warranty calls and follow-up service.
Do you handle Sun City HOA paperwork?
Yes. Sun City Recreation Centers / RCSC requires architectural review for exterior work and certain interior changes. We provide AZ ROC #341113, certificate of insurance, scope of work, and start/finish dates. We've been through the process dozens of times and keep the documentation moving.
Is cabinet refacing a good fit for Sun City homes?
Excellent fit. Most Sun City homes were built between 1960 and 1980 with cabinet boxes that are still structurally sound — they just need new doors, drawer fronts, and veneer. Refacing finishes in about a week and runs $4,500–$9,000 vs $15,000+ for full replacement. Our most-requested Sun City scope by a wide margin.
Do you do walk-in tub conversions and curbless showers?
Yes — both are top requests in Sun City. Walk-in tub: $7,500–$14,000. Curbless (zero-entry) tile shower: $7,500–$13,000. Both age-in-place ready, both completed in 5–10 working days on-site.
What materials do you recommend for Sun City kitchens?
Quartz countertops (zero maintenance, never seal) paired with cabinet refacing in painted white or warm neutral. For showers, large-format porcelain with a single accent niche — easier to clean than tile mosaics. We bring real samples to your home so you can hold them against your existing space.
How quickly can you start a project after I sign?
From signed contract: refacing 3 weeks, semi-custom cabinets 5–7 weeks, fully custom 6–10 weeks, countertop installation 2–3 weeks, tile shower 1–2 weeks. We don't double-book — your dates hold once we schedule.
Do you handle countertops, cabinets, AND tile, or just one?
All of it, plus flooring, millwork, and full kitchen and bath remodels. Self-performing multiple trades is the reason our pricing is competitive — you don't pay for trade coordination across separate contractors.
How does payment and financing work?
Standard schedule is 30% at contract, 40% at install start, 30% at completion. We accept cash, credit, check, and offer financing through our finance partners — most projects can be financed in 1–2 minutes during the in-home estimate.
Are you licensed and insured?
Yes — Arizona ROC #341113, fully bonded and insured. Our installation crews are background-checked. We provide license, insurance certificate, and scope of work in writing before any work starts.
