Sun City Flooring FAQs

How much to replace carpet with LVP in Sun City?
1,000 sq ft typical Sun City home: $6,500–$10,000 installed. Includes carpet removal and disposal, subfloor prep, vapor barrier where required, Lions Floor SPC or LVP, transitions, baseboard reset, quarter-round.
Can we do it while we're up north for summer?
Yes. Standard practice. Key access, daily photo updates, walkthrough on your return. Insured for unattended-home work.
Is SPC or LVP better for aging-in-place?
SPC for almost all Sun City homes. Hardest wear surface, doesn't dent under walker wheels or wheelchair tires, eliminates carpet trip hazards, low-glare matte finishes available.
RCSC approval needed?
RCSC (Recreation Centers of Sun City) handles community-wide review for substantial work. Floor-only swaps rarely require review. We provide documentation when needed.
How quickly can we start in Sun City?
Sun City calls get scheduling priority — we're 8 minutes away. Typical: in-home estimate within 48 hours of call, install starts 2 weeks later.
Best Lions Floor product line for Sun City?
We typically recommend District or Lone Star Spirit SPC for whole-house. Both have wood-look options that brighten the home and matte finishes that minimize glare for aging eyes.
