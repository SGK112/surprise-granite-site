Common Questions

Sun City Cabinet FAQs

How much do custom cabinets cost in Sun City?
Sun City custom cabinet pricing in 2026: stock $4,000–$8,000, semi-custom $10,000–$20,000, fully custom $20,000–$45,000 for an average 25-linear-foot kitchen. Refacing $4,500–$9,000. Our West Valley shop overhead is 15-30% lower than central Sun City fabricators — savings pass through to your quote.
Do you serve all the Sun City Rec Center areas?
Yes — we work all of Sun City proper plus the surrounding areas: Bell Rec, Lakeview, Marinette, Sundial, Mountain View, Fairway, Oakmont. About 5 miles (10 min) from our Surprise shop via Bell Rd.
Can you handle Sun City HOA approval paperwork?
Yes. We provide AZ ROC #341113, certificate of insurance naming the HOA as additional insured if required, scope of work, and start/finish dates. RCSC architectural review packet is templated. We file ROC, COI, scope of work, and timeline so you don't lose weeks waiting.
How long does a Sun City cabinet project take?
Refacing: ~3 weeks total (2-week material lead + 3-5 days install). Stock or semi-custom: 5-7 weeks. Fully custom: 6-10 weeks. We schedule countertop templating immediately after cabinet install so you're not stuck without counters.
Will refacing actually look like new cabinets in a 50-year-old Sun City kitchen?
Yes, if the boxes are sound — and in Sun City, they almost always are. The 1970s plywood and solid-wood box construction is often better than today's particle-board big-box cabinets. New doors, new veneer panels, new hardware = the kitchen reads as new.
Do you work with Sun City's RCSC (Rec Centers of Sun City)?
Yes. Sun City is governed differently than HOA communities — RCSC manages the recreation centers and there's a community-wide review for substantial work. We provide the documentation and scope packet RCSC needs when applicable.
Can you accommodate aging-in-place modifications during a cabinet refresh?
Yes. ADA-spec'd lower cabinet pull-outs, lever-handle hardware, lowered uppers (we can drop them 3–4 inches), under-counter knee clearance — all of this rolls into the cabinet scope at modest additional cost.
How long is install — we don't want our kitchen torn up for weeks?
Refacing install is 3–5 days; you're without the kitchen sink for 1 day. Full cabinet replacement is 4–7 days of install. Both timelines are predictable because we self-perform.
