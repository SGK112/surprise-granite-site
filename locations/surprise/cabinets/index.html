Common Questions

Surprise Cabinet FAQs

How much do custom cabinets cost in Surprise, AZ?
For an average-sized Surprise kitchen (around 25 linear feet of cabinetry), expect $4,000–$8,000 for stock, $10,000–$20,000 for semi-custom, and $20,000–$45,000 for fully custom with premium finishes. Fixed written quote after we measure on-site — no add-ons later.
Is cabinet refacing common in Surprise homes?
Very. A lot of Sun City Grand, Sun City West, and original Surprise homes have structurally sound 90s/2000s cabinet boxes that just need new doors, drawer fronts, and veneer. Refacing finishes in about a week and runs 40–60% less than full replacement.
Do you install cabinets I bought from IKEA or Home Depot?
Yes — installation only on owner-supplied cabinets is one of our common scopes. Pricing is per linear foot. We'll review your order list before scheduling so nothing's missing on install day.
How long does a Surprise cabinet install take?
Refacing 3–5 days on-site after a 2-week material lead. Stock or semi-custom: 2–4 weeks lead + 3–5 days install. Fully custom: 6–10 weeks total. We schedule countertop templating right after cabinet install so you're not stuck without counters.
