Common Questions

Vistancia FAQs

Do you specialize in Vistancia floor plans?
Yes. We've remodeled across the Village, Blackstone, and Trilogy at Vistancia. We know the Shea, Pulte, and David Weekley floor plans by name — the 10' and 11' ceilings, the typical island sizes, and the standard cabinet specs. That means cleaner bids, fewer surprises, and faster builds.
How does Vistancia's HOA approval work for remodels?
The Vistancia Community Association ARC (Architectural Review Committee) handles exterior changes and certain interior scopes. We submit AZ ROC #341113, certificate of insurance, project drawings, and the start/completion timeline. We've gone through the process dozens of times and keep the docs moving so you don't lose weeks waiting.
What's the typical Vistancia kitchen remodel cost?
Most Vistancia kitchens land between $30,000 and $75,000. Mid-range refacing + new quartz counters: $14,000–$22,000. Custom cabinets + quartzite + premium hardware + tile floor: $45,000–$80,000. Trilogy homes tend toward the lower end (smaller footprints), Blackstone toward the upper end (larger custom homes).
Can you do whole-home flooring in Vistancia?
Yes — it's one of our most-requested Vistancia jobs. Typical scope: 24x48 wood-look porcelain through living/kitchen/dining/halls, engineered hardwood or premium LVP in bedrooms, real natural stone in primary shower. 2,800–4,200 sq ft homes finish in 8–14 working days.
Do you do Trilogy at Vistancia walk-in shower conversions?
Yes — the most popular Trilogy project we run. Curbless tile shower with linear drain, frameless glass, built-in bench, and grab-bar blocking pre-installed. Ages-in-place ready while looking like a luxury upgrade. Typical timeline: 7–10 days, $7,500–$13,000.
