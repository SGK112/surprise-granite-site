Common Questions

Vistancia Flooring FAQs

Trilogy HOA approval for flooring?
Trilogy at Vistancia design review board has standards for substantial work. Interior flooring usually doesn't require approval; we provide documentation if asked.
Quiet hours for Trilogy install?
Yes — no power tools before 8am or after 5pm. We schedule install windows accordingly.
Vistancia pricing?
Lions Floor SPC/LVP: $5–9/sq ft installed. Whole 1,500 sq ft Vistancia home typical: $9,000–$14,000.
Lead time for Vistancia?
2–3 weeks contract to install. Same-week measure visits common since we're 18 minutes away.
Can you match a sample I already have?
Bring it to the in-home template. We'll match to a Lions Floor product line within color family. Exact matches across brands rarely happen; we'll be honest about the visual delta.
Builder LVP from 2010 — really need to replace?
Honest answer: depends on appearance and resale plans. Functionally, builder LVP from 2008–2015 has 3–8 years of life left if undamaged. Aesthetically, the wood-look styles from that era are dated. Replace for resale or your own enjoyment.
