Sun City West Cabinet FAQs

How much do custom cabinets cost in Sun City West?
Sun City West custom cabinet pricing in 2026: stock $4,000–$8,000, semi-custom $10,000–$20,000, fully custom $20,000–$45,000 for an average 25-linear-foot kitchen. Refacing $4,500–$9,000. Our West Valley shop overhead is 15-30% lower than central Sun City West fabricators — savings pass through to your quote.
Do you serve all the Sun City West Rec Center areas?
Yes — we work all of Sun City West proper plus the surrounding areas: R.H. Johnson Rec, Beardsley Rec, Kuentz Rec, Echo Mesa, Stardust, Pebblebrook. About 8 miles (12 min) from our Surprise shop via Bell Rd.
Can you handle Sun City West HOA approval paperwork?
Yes. We provide AZ ROC #341113, certificate of insurance naming the HOA as additional insured if required, scope of work, and start/finish dates. PORA architectural review packet is templated. We file ROC, COI, scope of work, and timeline so you don't lose weeks waiting.
How long does a Sun City West cabinet project take?
Refacing: ~3 weeks total (2-week material lead + 3-5 days install). Stock or semi-custom: 5-7 weeks. Fully custom: 6-10 weeks. We schedule countertop templating immediately after cabinet install so you're not stuck without counters.
How is Sun City West different from Sun City for cabinet work?
Newer housing stock — 1980s to 90s instead of 1960s/70s. Slightly larger kitchens. The cabinet boxes in Sun City West are usually birch or laminated particleboard rather than the older solid-wood stock in Sun City; both reface well. The cost and timeline are essentially identical.
Do you work with PORA (Property Owners and Residents Association) requirements?
Yes. PORA oversees community standards in Sun City West. For cabinet work that's purely interior, PORA approval is rarely required, but for any work affecting exterior or window appearances we provide the documentation packet.
Can we coordinate around scheduled community events and tournaments?
Yes. Sun City West has heavy community-event scheduling — golf tournaments, performances, holiday events. We work around homeowner availability without issue.
