Why Scottsdale Homeowners Hire Us for Flooring

Scottsdale flooring runs premium. McCormick Ranch mid-century homes get full hardwood refinishes or wide-plank European oak installs. McDowell Mountain Ranch and Grayhawk family homes want engineered hardwood plus tile in wet areas. DC Ranch and Silverleaf custom builds spec exotic — wide-plank rift-cut white oak, large-format porcelain, hand-scraped walnut, sometimes Lions Floor District Max for back-of-house and laundry. We come from Surprise, 35–45 minutes via the 101 — competitive on full-scope projects, not on small jobs.

McCormick Ranch hardwood refinish

1970s McCormick Ranch homes often have original red oak or oak-strip that's worth saving. Sand to bare wood, restain (often a darker custom mix to modernize), three coats water-based poly. Preserves the original character. $4–$7/sq ft.

Grayhawk & DC Ranch wide-plank install

Grayhawk and DC Ranch projects spec wide-plank European white oak, 7–9 inch boards, often rift-cut for grain stability. Lead time 4–8 weeks for material. Install 5–8 days for whole-house.

Silverleaf large-format porcelain

Silverleaf and Desert Mountain projects specify large-format porcelain (24x48 or larger) for great-room continuity. Two-person delivery, A-frame transport, careful cutting. Premium scope.

Scottsdale Flooring FAQs

Why hire a Surprise contractor for Scottsdale flooring?
For full-scope multi-room or whole-house projects. We're price-competitive vs Scottsdale-overhead contractors and self-perform multiple trades, which saves coordination headaches. For one-room small projects, a closer Scottsdale contractor is usually a better fit.
Pricing for Scottsdale projects?
Same as our wider service area, no Scottsdale surcharge: SPC/LVP $5–9/sq ft, hardwood $9–18/sq ft (premium European oak higher), engineered $9–16/sq ft, large-format porcelain $9–14/sq ft.
Lions Floor in Scottsdale?
Yes — we install Lions Floor across Scottsdale. District Max LVP is popular for back-of-house and laundry rooms; SPC for high-traffic areas paired with hardwood in formal spaces.
Old Town historic district approval?
Old Town Scottsdale has historic / overlay-district review. Flooring changes that don't affect exterior typically don't require approval, but substantial scope sometimes does. We handle documentation in the format Scottsdale's historic preservation office requires.
Hand-scraped or distressed hardwood?
Yes. We source hand-scraped, wire-brushed, and reactive-stain finishes through our distributor network. Lead time 4–8 weeks. Premium pricing $14–$22/sq ft installed.
Can you do hardwood and Lions Floor SPC in the same house?
Yes — and it's a common Scottsdale strategy. Hardwood in formal living and bedrooms, SPC or premium LVP in laundry, mudroom, and kitchen prep zones. We coordinate transitions for a flush look.
