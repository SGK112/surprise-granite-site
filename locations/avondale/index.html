Avondale FAQs

Do you serve Garden Lakes and Coldwater Ridge in Avondale?
Yes — both are core service areas, about 18–22 minutes from our Surprise showroom. We've remodeled kitchens, baths, and full-home flooring throughout Garden Lakes, Coldwater Ridge, Rancho Santa Fe, and Cambridge Estates. We know the typical builder cabinet specs and standard slab footprints in each.
What's the most common Avondale remodel scope?
Counters + backsplash + flooring is the high-ROI sweet spot. Most Avondale homeowners stay 5+ years and want upgrades that read luxury without the custom-cabinet price tag. Quartz counters + 24x48 wood-look porcelain runs $14,000–$22,000 and transforms the space.
Do you do flooring in Avondale?
Yes — tile, hardwood/LVP, and natural stone. Most Avondale homes get 24x48 wood-look porcelain in main living areas and engineered hardwood or premium LVP in bedrooms. We sequence demo, install, and trim so a 2,000–3,000 sq ft home is done in 7–10 working days.
How much does an Avondale kitchen remodel cost?
Most Avondale kitchens land between $18,000 and $45,000. Refacing + counters + backsplash: $14,000–$22,000. Full custom kitchen with cabinets, counters, and tile: $30,000–$50,000. We quote on the spot during the in-home estimate.
Can you handle the Avondale HOA paperwork?
Yes. Most Avondale HOAs are reasonable about interior remodels, but some require notification or a permit copy. We provide AZ ROC #341113, certificate of insurance, scope packet, and timeline — already templated for the most common Avondale communities.
How quickly can you start a project after I sign?
From signed contract: refacing 3 weeks, semi-custom cabinets 5–7 weeks, fully custom 6–10 weeks, countertop installation 2–3 weeks, tile shower 1–2 weeks. We don't double-book — your dates hold once we schedule.
Do you handle countertops, cabinets, AND tile, or just one?
All of it, plus flooring, millwork, and full kitchen and bath remodels. Self-performing multiple trades is the reason our pricing is competitive.
How does payment and financing work?
Standard schedule is 30% at contract, 40% at install start, 30% at completion. We accept cash, credit, check, and offer financing through our finance partners.
Are you licensed and insured?
Yes — Arizona ROC #341113, fully bonded and insured. Crews are background-checked. We provide license, insurance certificate, and scope of work in writing.
