Frequently Asked Questions About Bathroom Accessories

What is the standard height for a towel bar?
The standard towel bar height is 48 inches from the floor for adults and 36 inches for children's bathrooms. For hand towel rings near a vanity, mount at 20-26 inches above the countertop. Towel bars should be at least 18 inches from the shower opening to keep towels dry.
How do I match bathroom hardware finishes?
For a cohesive look, match your towel bars, robe hooks, and toilet paper holders to your faucet finish. Our KIBI bathroom hardware sets come in matching brushed gold, brushed nickel, matte black, and chrome finishes. Buying a 4-piece set ensures perfect finish matching across all bathroom accessories.
How do I install bathroom hardware?
Most bathroom hardware installs with a mounting bracket and two screws. Use wall anchors for drywall or drill directly into studs for the strongest hold. Towel bars support 15-20 lbs when properly anchored. Our hardware includes all mounting hardware and templates. For tile walls, use a diamond-tip drill bit to prevent cracking.
Is it better to buy individual pieces or a bathroom hardware set?
A 4-piece hardware set (towel bar, towel ring, toilet paper holder, robe hook) offers the best value and guarantees matching finishes. Individual pieces cost $49-$70 each, while a complete set runs $149-$199, saving 20-30%. Sets are ideal for new bathrooms or full renovations.
What are the trending bathroom accessory styles for 2026?
In 2026, matte black and brushed gold remain the most popular bathroom hardware finishes. Mixed metals (pairing gold fixtures with black hardware) are trending. Minimalist geometric designs from the Cube collection complement modern bathrooms, while the Circular collection suits transitional and traditional spaces.
