Frequently Asked Questions About Flooring

How much does luxury vinyl flooring cost per square foot?
Luxury vinyl plank (LVP) flooring at Surprise Granite starts at $2.99 per square foot for standard options and up to $4.99/sqft for premium hardwood styles. Pro members save up to 35% off retail pricing. Flooring is sold by the box (22 sqft per box) for easy project planning.
Is luxury vinyl plank flooring waterproof?
Yes! All MSI luxury vinyl plank flooring in our collection is 100% waterproof with a rigid SPC core. This makes LVP ideal for kitchens, bathrooms, laundry rooms, and basements. The waterproof core prevents moisture damage, warping, and mold growth even in high-humidity environments.
How many boxes of flooring do I need?
Measure your room's length and width in feet, multiply to get square footage, then add 10% for waste. Divide the total by 22 (sqft per box) and round up. For example, a 12x15 ft room = 180 sqft + 18 sqft waste = 198 sqft, which needs 9 boxes. Use our flooring calculator for exact numbers.
Can I install LVP over existing flooring?
In many cases, yes. LVP can be installed over existing hard surfaces like tile, concrete, and hardwood as long as the subfloor is level (within 3/16" per 10 feet), clean, and structurally sound. It should NOT be installed over carpet. Our click-lock LVP is designed for easy floating installation without glue.
