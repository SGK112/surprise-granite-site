Aria FAQs

Is Aria really free?
Yes. Aria is free to use, no signup required. She's our way of making design help available 24/7 without you having to wait for business hours or pick up the phone.
What can Aria actually help with?
Pricing ranges for any project (countertops, cabinets, tile, full remodels in Phoenix metro), material recommendations based on your style and budget, comparing options like quartz vs granite or refacing vs replacement, scheduling a real in-home estimate, and answering questions about our process, warranty, and ROC license.
Voice or text?
Both. Aria works in voice mode (talk naturally and she responds out loud) or text chat. Voice is faster for back-and-forth questions; text is better when you're looking up specifics or saving info.
Can Aria book my estimate?
Yes. If you tell her you want an in-home estimate, she'll capture your name, address, and project type and pass it to our scheduling team. You'll get a confirmation call within one business day.
Will my conversation be saved?
Conversations are stored briefly to improve Aria's responses but never sold or shared with advertisers. If you book an estimate, the project details get attached to your lead so our team can pick up where you left off.
