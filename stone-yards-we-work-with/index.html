How Slab Selection Actually Works

From Phone Call to Marked Slab in 5 Days

Day 1

Free In-Home Estimate

We come out with sample chips, measure with a laser, and quote on the spot. We narrow the conversation to 2-3 stone categories that fit your scope.

Day 2

Yard Recommendation

Based on what you want — exotic quartzite, premium quartz, budget-friendly granite — we tell you which 1-3 yards to hit first.

Day 3-4

Slab Walk

We meet you at the yard. Walk the racks together. Hold your sample chips against the actual slabs. Mark your top 3 with the yard's hold tag.

Day 5

Final Pick + Hold

Pick THE slab. We coordinate the yard hold and pickup. Your slab is now reserved with your name on it — no one else can take it.

Day 6-8

Templating + Fabrication

Digital laser template of your space. Slab cut to spec on our in-house CNC. Edge profile applied, hand-polished.

Day 9-10

Install + Walkthrough

Install in a single day. Plumbing reconnect, sealant on natural stone, final walkthrough. Total project: 7-10 days estimate to done.

Common Questions

Slab Selection FAQs

Why doesn't Surprise Granite have its own slab yard?
On purpose. Single-yard contractors lock you into their inventory — if your perfect slab isn't in their racks, you compromise. We are a stone fabricator and general contractor, not a slab distributor. We send you to whichever Phoenix yard has the slab that's right for your project. Free service. The result: you get the EXACT piece going into your kitchen, not the closest one we happen to stock.
What stone yards does Surprise Granite work with?
Ten major Phoenix-area stone yards: Arizona Tile, MSI Surfaces, Cosentino (Silestone, Dekton, Sensa), Daltile, Cactus Stone, Bolder Image Stone, ESI, Aracruz, Architectural Surfaces, and Classic Surfaces. Different yards specialize in different materials and price points — we know which yard to send you to first based on what you're looking for.
Do I have to walk the yard with you?
No, but most customers want to. Holding a real slab in person is the only way to know if it's the right piece. We meet you at the yard, narrow the selection together, and mark the exact slab. If you can't make it in person, we send slab photos with our notes for your approval.
Does it cost extra to walk multiple yards?
No. Slab walks are included in every countertop project at no additional cost. We will visit one yard or six — whatever it takes to find the slab you want.
Where are the major Phoenix stone yards located?
Most are in central Phoenix, Tempe, or south Phoenix — within 30–45 minutes of our Surprise shop. Arizona Tile and MSI both have multiple Phoenix metro locations. Cosentino's Phoenix center is in Tempe. We coordinate yard hours and routing so we can hit 2–3 yards in a single afternoon if needed.
