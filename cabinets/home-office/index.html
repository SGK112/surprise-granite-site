FAQ

Home-office questions.

How much does a custom home office cost?
Wall-to-wall built-in desk with cabinetry: $6,000–$18,000. Media walls with cabinets: $8,000–$25,000. Fixed written quote after free in-home measure.
Can you match existing trim?
Yes. Shop-painted or stained to match your baseboards, casings, and crown. The built-in should look original to the house.
Can you add power and cables?
Yes — grommets, in-cabinet outlets, cord channels, device chargers. We plan it during design and coordinate with your electrician if new circuits are needed.
