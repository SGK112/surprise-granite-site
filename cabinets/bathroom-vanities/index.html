FAQ

Vanity questions.

How much does a new vanity cost in Phoenix?
Single vanity with stone, sink, faucet: $1,800–$4,500 installed. Double vanity: $3,500–$8,500. Custom or floating: $5,000–$12,000+.
Can you match my kitchen cabinets?
Yes — same door style, finish, and hardware. One of the biggest tells of a well-done home.
What stone tops work for bathrooms?
Quartz is most popular (zero maintenance, non-porous, kid-proof). Granite works great. Marble is stunning but needs sealing and careful cleaner choice. Browse stone options.
Do you install floating vanities?
Yes — needs wall blocking behind the drywall and adjusted plumbing rough-in. We handle both during install or coordinate with your plumber.
