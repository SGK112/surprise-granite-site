FAQ

Answers before you pick up the phone.

How much do new kitchen cabinets cost in Phoenix?
Stock cabinets for an average Phoenix kitchen start around $4,000–$8,000 installed. Semi-custom runs $10,000–$20,000. Fully custom with premium finishes and designer hardware is $20,000–$45,000+. We give you a fixed written quote after a free in-home measure.
Is cabinet refacing worth it?
If your cabinet boxes are solid, yes. Refacing keeps the existing frames and swaps the doors, drawer fronts, and veneer — cutting cost by 40–60% vs new and finishing in about a week. Works great on 90s oak kitchens, dated thermofoil, and builder-grade cabinets common in Sun City / Surprise homes.
Do you install cabinets AND countertops together?
That's most of our projects. We measure, supply, and install both so there's one point of accountability and the countertop template lines up with the day cabinets go in. No gap between trades.
Do you do commercial cabinetry?
Yes — restaurant back-of-house, office break rooms, medical/dental exam casework, retail fixtures, multi-family unit kitchens. We hold the Arizona ROC license and can schedule around business hours so you don't lose operating days.
How long does a cabinet project take?
Refacing: 3–5 days on-site after about 2 weeks for materials. New stock/semi-custom: 2–4 weeks lead + 3–5 days install. Fully custom: 6–10 weeks depending on finish and hardware. Commercial timelines depend on scope and site access — published in writing before we start.
What cities do you serve?
Entire Phoenix metro: Surprise, Peoria, Sun City, Sun City West, Glendale, Phoenix, Scottsdale, Goodyear, Avondale, Buckeye, Waddell, El Mirage, Litchfield Park, Youngtown. We travel outside the metro for commercial work — just call.
