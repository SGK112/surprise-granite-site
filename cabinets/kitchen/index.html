FAQ

Kitchen cabinet questions.

How much do new kitchen cabinets cost in Phoenix?
Stock: $4,000–$8,000 installed. Semi-custom: $10,000–$20,000. Fully custom: $20,000–$45,000+. Add countertops: $2,500–$7,500 depending on material.
Can I keep my layout but get new cabinets?
Yes — a 'same-footprint' replacement is the fastest path. No plumbing or electrical changes. Usually 1–2 weeks on-site after lead time.
Do you handle the countertops, sink, and faucet too?
Yes — cabinets, countertops, sink, faucet, backsplash, and disposal handled by us. Tile we sub to a trusted local crew or partner with our preferred installers.
