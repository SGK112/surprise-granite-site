FAQ

Custom questions.

How much do custom cabinets cost?
Custom kitchen cabinetry in Phoenix typically runs $20,000–$45,000+ depending on wood species, door style, hardware, and scope. Inset doors, painted/glazed finishes, and full pantries push the price up. Fixed written quote after the design consult.
What's the difference between custom and semi-custom?
Semi-custom uses standard cabinet sizes with some finish and hardware choices. Full custom lets you change any dimension, any door style, any wood, and any finish. Odd angles, tall ceilings, specific storage needs — that's where custom pays for itself.
Can I see it before you build?
Yes. We produce 3D renders of the space so you know what every view looks like. Revisions are free during the design phase.
Do you also handle countertops?
Yes — that's our specialty. Countertops templated and installed by the same team. See stone options.
