Our Story

Surprise Granite began with a simple vision: to bring exceptional quality and craftsmanship to every Arizona home. As a locally owned and operated business, we understand the unique needs of our community and take pride in transforming houses into dream homes.

Serving the Phoenix metro area to job sites across the Phoenix metro area, our team delivers personalized service that sets us apart. We're not just contractors we're your neighbors, committed to excellence in every countertop we install and every room we remodel.

What Sets Us Apart

While others focus on volume, we focus on you. Every project receives the attention of our experienced project managers, ensuring seamless communication from initial consultation to final installation.

Our Commitment

Quality isn't just a buzzword it's our foundation. We source premium granite, quartz, marble, and porcelain from trusted suppliers, ensuring your investment stands the test of time.

Our skilled fabricators and installers bring decades of combined experience to every project. Whether it's a simple countertop replacement or a complete kitchen transformation, we approach each job with the same dedication to perfection.

We believe in transparency, fair pricing, and exceeding expectations. That's why over 400 families have trusted us with their homes, and why our customers consistently rate us 4.6 stars and above.

Our Core Values

Quality First

Premium materials, expert installation, and meticulous attention to detail in every project we undertake.

Local Expertise

Deep understanding of Arizona homes and lifestyles, with convenient in-home consultations and a team you can trust.

Clear Communication

Transparent pricing, realistic timelines, and constant updates throughout your remodeling journey.

Efficient Service

Quick turnaround times without compromising quality, minimizing disruption to your daily life.

Lifetime Support

Our relationship doesn't end at installation. We stand behind our work for as long as you own your home.

Value Driven

Competitive pricing through strong supplier relationships, passing savings directly to our customers.
