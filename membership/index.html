Frequently Asked Questions

How much can I really save?
Savings depend on your membership level and what you buy. Homeowner accounts save up to 3% off retail. Pro members save up to 35%. Trade professionals save 25-30%, and fabricators get true wholesale pricing. On a typical kitchen countertop project, Pro members can save $500-$1500.
Can I cancel my Pro membership anytime?
Yes, you can cancel your Pro or Fabricator membership at any time. There are no contracts or cancellation fees. Your membership will remain active through the end of your billing period.
What's required for trade verification?
For Contractor/Designer accounts, we require proof of business (contractor license, business license, or resale certificate). Verification typically takes 1-2 business days. Once verified, you get trade pricing with no monthly fee.
Do you offer volume discounts?
Yes! Fabricator Partners get access to additional volume-based pricing on large orders. Contact your dedicated rep for quotes on container or pallet quantities.
Can I upgrade my membership later?
Absolutely. You can upgrade from Homeowner to Pro at any time. If you become a licensed contractor or fabricator, you can apply for trade verification to access even better pricing.
Is my pricing visible to others?
No. Your pricing is completely private. Each account only sees their own tier pricing. We don't display comparison pricing between tiers to protect everyone's margins.
