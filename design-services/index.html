Common Questions

Design Consultation FAQs

Is the design consultation really free?
Yes. Free in-home consultation, no obligation. Most consultations run 60-90 minutes and include measuring, sample review, layout discussion, and a verbal pricing range. A formal written quote follows within a week if you want to move forward.
Do you bring 3D renderings to the consultation?
Renderings are produced after the consultation, not at it. The consultation is for measuring, talking through layout, and selecting materials. Once we have your selections we produce 3D renderings (kitchen elevations, perspective views, finish boards) within 5-7 business days so you can see exactly what your kitchen will look like before signing a contract.
What samples will you bring?
Cabinet door samples (4-6 styles in your preferred finish), countertop chips (granite, quartz, marble in the colors you indicated interest in), tile samples for backsplash and flooring, hardware finish options, and edge profile examples for stone counters. We tailor what we bring to what you tell us upfront so the appointment is productive.
Are you really the top designer in the West Valley?
We back the claim with 400+ completed West Valley projects, 4.6-star Google rating, and the ROC license (341113) most of our competitors don't carry. The West Valley specifically — Surprise, Peoria, Sun City, Sun City West, Sun City Grand, Glendale, Goodyear, Litchfield — is our home market and where most of our active jobsites sit on any given week.
Do you handle all-new cabinetry or just countertops?
Both. All-new cabinetry is one of our most-quoted scopes — custom builds, semi-custom from KraftMaid/Schrock/Wellborn, and refacing for the West Valley homes where the boxes are still sound. Countertops typically follow the cabinet install. Done together you save 2-3 weeks of disruption vs sequencing them as separate projects.
